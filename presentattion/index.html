PROPOSAL 1

The Congestion Pricing Verdict

A year later, did NYC's policy work as promised, or did it create unintended consequences for the outer boroughs?

Why it's a winner: Extremely high public relevance. A perfect blend of data science and public policy analysis.

Proposal 1: Technical Deep Dive

Data Sources

  • NYC Open Data (Traffic, Air Quality, 311)
  • MTA Turnstile & OMNY Data
  • US Census Data

Core Techniques

  • Causal Inference (Diff-in-Diff)
  • Time-Series Forecasting (ARIMA)
  • Geospatial Analysis (GIS)

Pros

  • High-quality public data is available
  • Advanced methodology is great for resumes
  • Huge potential for media attention

Cons

  • Causal inference is complex to do right
  • Results could be politically sensitive

PROPOSAL 2

The "Office Apocalypse" Algorithm

Can we build an ML model to predict which commercial buildings are at the highest risk of long-term vacancy?

Why it's a winner: Solves a multi-billion dollar problem. A tangible, predictive tool that showcases modern ML skills.

Proposal 2: Technical Deep Dive

Data Sources

  • NYC PLUTO Building Data
  • Commercial Real Estate APIs (Zillow, etc.)
  • MTA Data (as a foot-traffic proxy)

Core Techniques

  • Supervised ML (XGBoost)
  • Advanced Feature Engineering
  • Interactive Dashboard/Visualization

Pros

  • Excellent portfolio piece
  • Very relevant to business/tech sectors
  • Creates a tangible, useful tool

Cons

  • High risk: Data acquisition is difficult
  • Success depends on finding good proxies

PROPOSAL 3

AI for Predicting Hate Crimes

Can we use NLP on social media to analyze hate speech and forecast which neighborhoods are at a higher risk of a real-world incident?

Why it's a winner: Highest potential for social impact. Uses state-of-the-art AI for a noble and highly relevant purpose.

Proposal 3: Technical Deep Dive

Data Sources

  • NYPD Hate Crimes Data
  • Social Media APIs (X/Twitter, Reddit)
  • ACLU / ADL Reports

Core Techniques

  • Natural Language Processing (BERT)
  • Hate Speech Classification
  • Time-Series Forecasting

Pros

  • Incredibly meaningful and impactful work
  • Uses cutting-edge NLP models
  • Strong potential for positive media

Cons

  • Highest risk: Major ethical concerns
  • Social media API access can be difficult
  • Requires deep NLP expertise
